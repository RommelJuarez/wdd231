Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Savage Beast

Reason: This name represents strength, determination, and unleashing one's full potential during workouts.

Optional Domain: savagebeastfit.com

Site Purpose

Savage Beast is a web app designed to help users track their weekly workout routines and create personalized exercise plans. It provides an easy-to-use platform for fitness enthusiasts of all levels to stay consistent, measure progress, and optimize their training.

Scenarios

  • How can I create a personalized workout plan based on my fitness goals?
  • Where can I log and track my weekly training progress?
  • What exercises should I include for strength training and endurance?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: (#9B51E0 - Vibrant Purple, used for headings and key elements)

Secondary Color: (#6C3483 - Dark Purple, used for backgrounds and highlights)

Accent 1: (#ffffff - White, used for text with balck backgrounds)

Accent 2: (#000000 - Black, used for background and dark mode base color)

Typography

Heading Font: Impact, sans-serif (for a bold and energetic feel)

Body Font: Roboto, sans-serif (for readability and modern style)
